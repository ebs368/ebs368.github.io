Contact Erica

Erica Snyder is an aspiring screenwriter studying Dramatic Writing at NYU Tisch School of the Arts. She is published in many online publications including Cosmoplitan.com, Seventeenmag.com, and Groknation.com. When not writing, she enjoys “normal” teenage activities — hanging out with friends, photographing New York City, and binge-watching Netflix (Prime or Hulu, too)!
